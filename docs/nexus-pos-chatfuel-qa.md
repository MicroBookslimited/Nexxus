# NEXXUS POS — Chatfuel Q&A Knowledge Base

> Comprehensive question-and-answer set designed to be imported into a Chatfuel
> bot (Messenger / WhatsApp / Instagram) as the AI / FAQ training dataset.
> Each block is a single intent. The **User asks** lines are training phrases
> (utterances). The **Bot replies** block is the answer to send back.
> Where helpful we keep answers short enough for messenger bubbles
> (≤ 640 characters per bubble); long answers are split into clearly numbered
> bubbles so they fit Facebook's message limits.

Brand voice: friendly, professional, concise, helpful. Currency JMD.
Always sign off with "— NEXXUS POS, Powered by MicroBooks" only on the first
bot reply of a conversation, not on every message.

## SECTION 4 — Selling at the Point of Sale

### Q21. How do I ring up a sale?
**User asks**:
- How do I make a sale?
- How do I sell something?
- Cashier mode

**Bot reply**:
On the POS screen:
1) Search by name or scan the barcode (or tap the product tile).
2) Adjust quantity with + / – or the on-screen numpad.
3) Tap the customer name (optional).
4) Pick a payment method.
5) Tap "Charge" to complete the sale and print/email the receipt.

### Q22. How do I scan barcodes?
**User asks**:
- How does barcode scanning work?
- Connect a scanner
- Scan products

**Bot reply**:
Plug in any USB or Bluetooth barcode scanner — they all act as a keyboard.
Just keep the cursor in the POS search bar and scan; the matching product
is added to the cart instantly. No drivers needed.

### Q23. How do I sell items by weight?

it to the cart at the POS, a weight modal pops up — enter the weight (e.g.
1.75 kg) and the line total is calculated automatically as price × weight.

## SECTION 5 — Inventory Management

### Q34. How does inventory tracking work?
**User asks**:
- How is stock tracked?
- Inventory deduction
- Auto stock update

**Bot reply**:
Stock is deducted automatically the moment a sale is completed. You can see
live stock levels on the Products page, get low-stock alerts by email, and
view a full Stock Movements log of every adjustment, sale, purchase or
transfer.

### Q35. Can I prevent overselling?
**User asks**:
- Can someone sell more than I have in stock?
- Negative inventory
- Block oversell

**Bot reply**:
Yes. NEXXUS POS uses an atomic stock check at checkout: if a cashier tries
to sell 5 units but only 3 are available, the sale is blocked with a clear
"Only 3 of <product> available" message. You can override per product if
you allow back-orders.

### Q36. How do I adjust stock manually?
**User asks**:
- Stock adjustment
- Damage / loss / shrinkage
- Stock count

**Bot reply**:
Go to Products → pick the item → "Adjust Stock". Enter the new quantity
and a reason (received, damaged, lost, count correction). The change is
logged and the cost-of-goods journal is updated automatically.

### Q37. How do I record a purchase from a supplier?
**User asks**:
- Add stock from supplier
- Record purchase
- Receive goods
- Vendor purchase

**Bot reply**:
Go to Vendors → New Purchase. Pick the supplier, add the items received and
their cost. Stock goes up, cost averages update, and a payable is recorded
in Accounts Payable so you don't forget to pay your vendor.

### Q38. Can I track ingredients and recipes?
**User asks**:
- Recipe management
- Track ingredients
- Manufacturing
- BOM

**Bot reply**:
Yes — perfect for restaurants and producers. Define ingredients in
Ingredients, build recipes in Recipes (e.g. 1 Burger = 1 patty + 1 bun +
2 slices cheese), and log a Production run. Selling the burger automatically
deducts the ingredient stock.

### Q39. Can I have multiple units of measure?
**User asks**:
- Buy in cases sell in units
- Multiple units
- Pack vs unit
- Purchase units

**Bot reply**:
Yes. On any product set up purchase units (e.g. buy by Case = 24 units) and
sale units (e.g. sell by Each, Pack of 6). Conversions happen automatically
so your stock count and cost stay accurate.

### Q40. How do low-stock alerts work?
**User asks**:
- Low stock alert
- Reorder notification
- Out of stock email

**Bot reply**:
Set a re-order point on each product (e.g. alert when stock < 10). The
system sends a daily email digest to owners listing every product below its
threshold so you know exactly what to restock.

### Q41. Can I transfer stock between locations?
**User asks**:
- Move stock between branches
- Stock transfer
- Inter-branch

**Bot reply**:
Yes. Go to Locations → Stock Transfer, pick the source and destination
branches, choose products and quantities. Stock moves from one to the other
and the transfer is logged for audit.

1. **AI rules import (recommended).** In Chatfuel open *Automate → AI* and
   for each Q above paste the User-asks lines into "If user says..." and the
   Bot-replies block into "Bot replies with...". Use the section headers as
   tags so reporting can group by topic.

2. **CSV import.** Convert the document with this structure:

   ```csv
   intent,utterances,reply
   "what_is_nexus","What is NEXXUS POS|Tell me about it|What does this app do","NEXXUS POS is an all-in-one cloud Point-of-Sale system..."
   ```

   Pipe-separate utterances and use straight quotes. Most teams generate
   the CSV from this markdown using a small script — ask support and we'll
   share one.

3. **Live agent handoff.** Set the keyword *"Talk to support"* (and synonyms
   like *human, agent, live person, real person*) to trigger Chatfuel's Live
   Chat / inbox handoff flow so a human can take over.

4. **Buttons / quick replies.** After the Greeting reply (Q91) attach quick
   replies for the top categories:  *Pricing*  *Features*  *Setup*
   *Support*. This keeps users on a happy path.

5. **Personalisation tokens.** Use Chatfuel's `{{first name}}` token in the
   greeting and farewell replies to make the bot feel personal.
